# LinkedIn Hook Formulas — Quick Reference

## Hook Types & Examples

### 1. Pattern Interrupt + Vulnerability
Opens with a relatable confession or counterintuitive statement.
- "Most weeks I ship 3 things and forget about them. This week I actually wrote them down."
- "I've been doing X wrong for 2 years. Here's what I changed."
- "I almost didn't post this because it sounds too simple."

### 2. Contrarian / Hot Take
Challenge a common belief.
- "Everyone says use Kubernetes for self-hosting. I disagree."
- "The 'best practice' everyone follows is actually making things worse."

### 3. List / Number
Scannable, promise specific value.
- "5 things I learned migrating from VMC to TrueNAS this week:"
- "3 bugs that took me 3 days each to find (and the 5-minute fix for each)"

### 4. Question
Engage curiosity.
- "What if the bottleneck isn't where you think it is?"
- "Why does everyone deploy on Friday and then wonder why Monday is painful?"

### 5. Behind the Scenes
Show the process, not just the result.
- "Here's what actually happens when you type 'hermes run' and hit Enter:"
- "A real incident, start to finish, from alert to fix in 20 minutes."

### 6. Myth-Busting
Debunk with evidence.
- "Self-hosting is dead. Except it isn't. Here's the data."
- "People say Go is verbose. They're reading the wrong Go."

### 7. Story Opener
Drop the reader into a moment.
- "Last Tuesday at 2am, a smoke test caught something I'd ignored for weeks."
- "I deleted 2,000 lines of code on a Friday afternoon. Here's why I'd do it again."

## Selection Rules

- **Monday/Friday (Build Log):** Use List or Story Opener
- **Tuesday (Teach):** Use Behind the Scenes or Question
- **Wednesday (Hot Take):** Use Contrarian or Myth-Busting
- **Thursday (Community):** Use Vulnerability or Story Opener
- Never use the same hook type two days in a row
- Rotate through at least 4 different types per week
