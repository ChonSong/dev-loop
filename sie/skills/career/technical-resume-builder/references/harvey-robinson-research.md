# Company Research: Harvey Robinson Pty Ltd

## Context
Boutique technology recruitment agency in Sydney. Screened for a Junior Systems Engineer role ($105-115k + super).

## Key Facts
- Founded April 2024 by Harvey Eckett + Laura Robinson (ex-Harvey Nash directors)
- ~3 employees, ~2,500 LinkedIn followers
- SEEK rating: 4.98/5.0 from 53 reviews (exceptional)
- No Glassdoor profile (too small)
- Registered ASIC company, active ABN/GST
- No major news coverage, no controversies

## Recruitment Focus
- Banking/Financial Services, Managed Service Providers, Microsoft Partners, Enterprise, FinTech, Government/Defence
- Roles: Infrastructure, Cyber Security, Software Engineering, AI, Product, Data
- Sectors: $1.2B banking transformation programs, AWS/GCP environments

## How to Approach
- This is a recruitment agency, not the direct employer — CV needs to impress the recruiter FIRST
- The agency forwards you to their unnamed client
- Recruiters scan in ~6 seconds; conciseness matters
- The CV should scream "infrastructure person" — every line relevant to systems engineering
- Recruiters look for: clear career narrative, keywords matching the job ad, proof you're placeable
