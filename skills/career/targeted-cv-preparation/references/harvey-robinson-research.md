# Harvey Robinson Research — June 2026

## Who They Are
Harvey Robinson Pty Ltd is a **boutique technology recruitment agency** (NOT a direct employer). Sydney-based (200 George Street), founded April 2024 by Harvey Eckett and Laura Robinson — both ex-Harvey Nash directors.

## Key Facts
- Size: ~3 employees, ~2,500 LinkedIn followers
- SEEK rating: **4.98/5.0** from 53 reviews (exceptional)
- No Glassdoor profile (too small)
- Clients: banking/FS, MSPs, Microsoft partners, enterprise, fintech, government/defence
- Active roles at time of research: ~20, including Junior Cloud Engineer ($115-120k), Azure Security Engineer, Network Engineers ($120-170k), Programme Manager ($1.2B banking transformation)

## Application Insight
This was an **agency-placed role**. The job ad said "email CV to info@harveyrobinson.com.au" — the CV had to impress the recruiter (Harvey Robinson) first before they would forward it to their unnamed client. Key signals the recruiter was looking for: home-lab/build-things mentality, automation/scripting, IT systems/infrastructure, cloud exposure.

## What Worked in the CV
- Leading with the **self-hosted infrastructure** story (9 Docker containers, Cloudflare, systemd) — directly matched their "home lab" culture ask
- Specific technical details from source code (nhooyr.io/websocket, HMAC-SHA256, NoNewPrivileges) — showed genuine depth
- Principle of Parsimony as a philosophy sidebar — differentiated from generic graduate CVs
- 1 page, tight formatting with <5% blank space

## What Wouldn't Have Worked
- Sending the generic Graduate CV — would have been lost in the applicant pool
- Including ML/Data Science projects — cuts the wrong direction for a systems/infra role
- Including casaos-agent or hermes-sync (explicitly excluded by the user)
