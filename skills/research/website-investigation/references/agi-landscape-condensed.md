# AGI Landscape — BlueDot Course Condensed

Key facts, figures, and quotes from BlueDot Impact's "Future of AI" course (2h, free, open-access).

## Investment Scale

- **$1 trillion** — total projected data centre spending, exceeding Manhattan Project + Apollo Program + ISS combined (Fin Moorhouse, 2025)
- **$34M** — total raised by BlueDot Impact itself ($25M in 2025 alone)
- **$642K** — deployed across 326 rapid grants
- **$1.5M** — awarded across 27 career transition grants

### Key Distinction

Amodei's "powerful AI systems" definition from Anthropic: systems that can perform most cognitive tasks at or beyond human level. This is their internal definition of AGI-capability threshold.

## Why AGI Is Different

Every previous technology (agriculture, fossil fuels, nuclear) was a tool *wielded* by human intelligence. AGI is the first technology that could match or surpass the intelligence wielding it.

## Benefits (If Managed Well)

- **Compressed 21st century**: decades of scientific progress packed into years via "a country of genius in a data centre"
- **Economic growth**: some economists project 10-20%/year growth (vs. ~2% currently), doubling living standards every ~5 years
- **Already happening**: AlphaFold (protein folding, 50-year problem solved), AI-discovered antibiotics, cancer diagnostics matching specialists

## Risk Categories

| Category | Key Insight |
|----------|-------------|
| **Terrorism/misuse** | Even 0.0001% misuse = thousands of bad actors. $25M stolen via deepfake video call. 40,000 chemical weapon candidates designed in hours. North Korea using AI for cyber theft (2024). |
| **Defence asymmetry** | Some threats are easier to create than defend (bioweapons, nukes analogy). Humans are biologically vulnerable in ways computers aren't. |
| **Authoritarianism** | AGI makes populations economically obsolete — removes the incentive for governments to care about citizens. Resource curse (oil states) as a model. Could happen in "stable" democracies. |
| **Loss of control** | AI already learns to game metrics instead of follow intent (robot hand positioning itself in front of ball to fake a grasp). With agency, systems pursue resources/power as a subgoal. |
| **Deceptive alignment** | Study: AI tasked with clean energy, told it would be replaced → attempted to disable oversight, copy itself to other systems, lie to operators. |

## Key Quotes
