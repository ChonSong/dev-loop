## Callout Placement Rules

1. **Above bars** — when the value itself is the story (e.g., "72% Use AI")
2. **Between columns** — when the gap/difference is the story (e.g., "44% Gap")
3. **Below the chart** — when the implication is the story (e.g., "Invest in training before technology")
4. **Overlaid on data point** — when a specific data point needs attention (e.g., callout box on 2026 bar)

## Transition Timing

| Transition | Duration | Effect |
|------------|----------|--------|
| Fade in | 0.5s | Smooth start |
| Hold | 7-9s | Readable time |
| Fade out | 0.5s | Smooth end |
| Crossfade | 0.5s | Between similar-layout segments |
| Black flash | 0.2s | Between different-panel segments |
