# LinkedIn Hook Library

Proven hook formats + post templates for B2B / dev / infra audiences.

## Hook Formulas (pick one per post)

### Pain/Panic
- "I spent [X hours/days] on [problem] so you don't have to."
- "The [tool/advice] everyone recommends is actually setting you back."

### Contrarian
- "Unpopular opinion: [common practice] is wrong."
- "Why I stopped using [popular tool] and switched to [your approach]."

### Before/After
- "Before: [status quo]. After: [your solution]. Here's the 3 steps:"
- "30 days ago I [old way]. Today I [new way]. The difference is [result]."

### Listicle
- "[X] things I wish I knew before building [thing]"
- "[X] tools I use daily as [role]"

### Story
- "Last week I made a mistake that cost me [X]. Here's what I learned:"
- "I was the only person at [event/company] who [unique thing]. Here's what happened:"

### Results
- "How I [achieved specific result] in [timeframe] using [stack]"
- "[Metric] improvement after switching from [A] to [B]"

### Question
- "What if [common assumption] is completely wrong?"
- "Why does everyone ignore [important thing]?"

## Post Templates

### Build Log (shipping updates)
```
[Tight 1-line summary of what shipped]

The problem:
[1-2 sentences on the pain point]

What I built:
[1-2 sentences on the solution]

Tech stack: [stack]

[Repository link or screenshot]

#opensource #[topic] #[stack-related hashtag]
```

### Teaching Post (explain a concept)
```
[Hook formula — pick from above]

[Concept] in 3 steps:

Step 1: [one sentence]
Step 2: [one sentence]
Step 3: [one sentence]

The key insight: [one sentence that makes them feel smarter]

Agree? Disagree? Share below
```

### Hot Take (opinion)
```
Unpopular opinion: [statement].

Here's why:

Reason 1
Reason 2
Reason 3

Now change my mind.
```

## CTA Options (end every post with one)
- "What's your approach? Share below"
- "Agree or disagree? Let me know."
- "Save this for later if you're building with [tool]."
- "Follow for more on [topic]."

Keep posts to 150-300 words. Short paragraphs. Line breaks between every 2-3 sentences.
