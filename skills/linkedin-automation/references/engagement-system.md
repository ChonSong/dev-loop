# LinkedIn Engagement System

Daily, weekly, and connection strategies for growing LinkedIn presence.

## Daily Ritual (15 minutes)

Morning:
1. Check notifications — reply to all comments on your posts within 4 hours
2. **Comment on 3 posts** from people in your network (see templates below)
3. **Post your content** (if scheduled for today)

Afternoon:
4. Check for new replies — respond within 2 hours
5. **1 connection request** with personalized note

## Comment Templates

### On technical/posts:
"Great breakdown of [specific thing]. I've been using [related tool/approach] for [angle] and found [brief insight]. Curious if you've tried [question]?"

### On opinion posts:
"Solid point on [specific thing]. My experience: [brief counterpoint or supporting data]. Would love to see how [related trend] affects this."

### On shipping/build logs:
"Shipped. Especially the part about [specific detail] — I hit the same issue with [related thing]. Did you consider [question/alternative]?"

**Rules:** Always add value (never just say "great post"). Ask a question or add a data point. 2-4 sentences max.

## Connection Request Templates

### Peer (same space):
"Hey [Name], saw your work on [specific thing]. I'm building in the same space ([your thing]) and would love to connect."

### After engaging with their content:
"[Name], your post on [topic] was [specific compliment]. Building something similar with [your angle]. Would be great to stay in touch."

### General:
"[Name], I'm refreshing my LinkedIn. We worked together on [context]. Would you be comfortable writing a brief recommendation? I can draft one for your review if that's easier."

## Target Personas
1. Indie hackers building AI tools
2. DevRel / community at infra companies (Fly.io, Coolify, TrueNAS, Docker)
3. Open-source maintainers in Go/Svelte/agent space
4. Homelab / self-hosting community
5. Hiring managers at agent/AI infrastructure companies

## Weekly Review (15 min, Monday)

Track in `/workspace/linkedin-audit/weekly-YYYY-MM-DD.md`:
- Posts made, engagement reach
- Comments given (target: 15/week)
- Connection requests sent (target: 5/week)
- Inbound DMs
- Notable interactions, milestones
- 3 improvements for next week
